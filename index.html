Website introduction

National Basketball Association (NBA), a men's Professional Basketball League composed of 30 professional teams in North America, is one of the four major professional sports leagues in the United States. The NBA is divided into the Eastern League and the Western League. Each league is divided into three divisions, each of which is composed of five teams. After each season, the NBA draft will be held before the beginning of the next season. After the draft, there will be Rookies of each team, NBA summer league and NBA preseason The NBA regular season usually starts in October, and the preseason includes NBA overseas games. In February, there was a special performance event, the NBA all star game. After the NBA regular season, the eastern and Western leagues entered the playoffs from the top eight, determined the eastern and Western champions, and advanced to the NBA finals. Favorite team

Atlanta Hawks

Atlanta Hawks is a professional basketball team located in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. It was founded in 1946 and joined the American men's basketball professional league (NBA) in 1949. Now it is a team participating in the Southeast Division of the Eastern League.

Charlotte Hornets

Charlotte Hornets is a professional basketball team located in Charlotte, North Carolina. Its predecessor Charlotte Bobcats was established in 2004 and joined the American men's basketball professional league (NBA). It is the youngest team in the league and is now the team participating in the East South Division of the Eastern League.

Miami Heat

The Miami heat is a professional basketball team located in Miami, Florida, USA. It was established in 1988 and joined the American men's basketball professional league (NBA). The main venue of the team is the American Airlines Center Stadium in downtown Miami. It is now a team participating in the Southeast Division of the Eastern League

Orlando Magic

Orlando Magic is a professional basketball team located in Orlando, Florida. It was founded in 1989 and joined the American men's basketball professional league (NBA). It is now a team participating in the Southeast Division of the Eastern League.
