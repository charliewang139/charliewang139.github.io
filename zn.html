NBA introduction

NBA details

National Basketball Association (NBA), a men's Professional Basketball League composed of 30 professional teams in North America, is one of the four major professional sports leagues in the United States, including Michael Jordan, Bill Russell, Kobe Bryant, Karim Abdul Jabbar, Wilt Chamberlain, Elvin Johnson, Larry Bird, Oscar Robertson and John havelichek , Rick Barry, John Stockton, Carl Malone, Elgin Baylor, David Robinson, Julius Owen, Shaquille O'Neal, Charles Buckley, Allen Iverson, LeBron James, Stephen curry, Chris Paul, James Harden, Yao Ming and other legendary stars.

The NBA is divided into the Eastern League and the Western League. Each league is divided into three divisions, each consisting of five teams. After the end of each season and before the beginning of the next season, NBA draft will be held. After the draft, there will be the rookie NBA Summer League of each team. NBA preseason and NBA regular season usually start in October. Preseason includes NBA overseas games. In February, there was a special performance event, the NBA all star game. After the NBA regular season, the eastern and Western leagues entered the playoffs from the top eight, determined the eastern and Western champions, and advanced to the NBA finals. The best player won the Bill Russell Award for the most valuable player in the NBA finals

At the beginning of the establishment of the league, there were only 11 teams, of which only 3 teams have continued to this day. They are warriors, Knicks and Celtics, and the other 8 teams have been dissolved in five years. On June 6, 1946, baa, the predecessor of NBA, was established in the United States. Preseason

It is a warm-up game for all NBA teams, because after the end of each season, there are considerable changes in the lineup of each team. In order to make each team run in the lineup, get familiar with the playing methods of their teams, determine the lineup of each team in the new season, and improve the communication between players and coaches, NBA holds several preseason games before the beginning of each season, So that they can devote themselves to the long regular season in a better state. In order to expand the NBA's global influence, about one-third of the preseason teams are held outside the United States

regular season

82 regular season games: 1: Play two games with teams in different leagues, one home and one away (a total of 30 games) 2: Play 4 games with teams in the same division, 2 home games each (16 games in total) 3: Play 4 games (24 in total) with 6 teams in different divisions of the same league and 3 games (12 in total) with 4 teams

All star game

As a traditional basketball feast, the NBA all star weekend has been held for 53 times. The original idea was put forward by HASK Cohen, the head of Public Relations Department of the NBA headquarters at that time, and the idea came from the All-Star game of the National Professional Baseball League. Naturally, the purpose is to further promote the NBA. New things are generally not optimistic, and the NBA All-Star game is no exception. Who can think of such luxury in the All-Star game 50 years later? At this time, Portland pioneer owner Walter Brown stood up and strongly supported the event. And the All-Star game didn't have so many tricks at that time. It was only an All-Star game. The starting lineup of the All-Star game was voted by fans all over the world and passed the NBA COM, NBA app, social media and other channels to vote. The substitute lineup of the All-Star game is voted by the head coaches of 30 teams. If the players cannot attend due to injury, the League president Adam Xiaohua will appoint a player from other players to supplement. If a starter is unable to attend due to injury, the coach of the all star game shall select a player from the bench. As of the start of the All-Star game, the head coaches of the best teams in the two major leagues served as the head coaches of their respective League All-Star teams.

playoff

The eastern and Western leagues enter the playoffs from the top eight respectively, based on the first to eighth, the second to seventh, and so on. Each round of the series adopts the game system of four wins in seven sets, and the team with the best record in the regular season has the home advantage. (2-2-1-1-1) the division champions of the two major leagues entered the finals, also winning four of the seven innings.
